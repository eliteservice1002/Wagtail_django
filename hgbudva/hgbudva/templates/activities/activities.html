{% blocktrans %} “Budvanska Rivijera” Hotel Group is recognized as a company that keeps up with the latest tourism trends, constantly examining the needs and demands of its guests. Through detailed research over the years, activity- and family-oriented vacations have emerged as the backbone of the company’s operations, which is why we are primarily oriented towards engaging tourists with various entertainment programs.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}A diverse list of activities had been compiled, designed, crafted and conducted by our entertainment team - a group of trained professionals in fields such as pedagogy, physical education, and psychology. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}These instructors carry classes of morning gymnastics, aqua aerobics, swimming, mini golf, beach volleyball, and some on demand activities (if possible to arrange). Firm supporters of active vacation and recreation programmes cherish the moments we create together, calling them “an opportunity to add more value to vacations” - all done through activities such as Zumba, Latin dance classes, yoga, and many more. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Sport Activities - Tennis Courts" %}

{% blocktrans %} Within the resort there is one of the most modern tennis centers in the region, which is due to the highest standards of construction and quality infrastructure, accommodation facilities and additional facilities. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Within the tennis center there are 11courts, 8 courts on clay, as well as three concrete courts, with all the accompanying facilities (locker rooms, showers, rest area, coffee bar). In addition to the tennis center for tennis fans, there is a modern gym, as well as a Jacuzzi and sauna that visitors of the center can use within the hotel "Aleksandar", only 50 m away from the center. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Tennis center "Slovenska plaža" within its offer offers the organization of individual, group, tourist and other tennis activities, as well as the possibility of individual and group engagement of tennis coaches, organization of sparring matches, rental of courts and tennis equipment and other various facilities. {% endblocktrans %}
