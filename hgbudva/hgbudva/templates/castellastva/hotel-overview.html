{% trans "Welcome to Hotel Castellastva" %}

{% blocktrans %} The establishment is located in the centre of Petrovac, a small coastal town with a natural ambience among pines and olive groves, snugged between rugged hills and overlooking the Adriatic sea. With only 150m walk to the beachfront, hotel Castellastva is an ideal place for families or elderly guests who look for relaxing vacations.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The hotel’s name derives from the Italian “castellastua”, which was Venetian fortress built in XVI century. Prior to that it was called Kastel Lastva, which means “Petrovac on the Sea”, and like this preserves and recalls the tradition of decades of tourist developments in the town. Its setting exudes warmth and comfort, making it the perfect fit for travelers who want a peaceful break. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} For a new Mediterranean story, the complex itself was significantly remodeled which can be easily noticed in the avant-garde, modern style of its new lobby, ambiental restaurant, and 185 well decorated rooms and suites. After completion, the once old building got to boast with one more star in its constellation of stars, levelling up and thus presenting new quality offers for an overall better stay. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "A 4-star Experience for Children" %}

{% blocktrans %} You’ll be immersed in a pallet of pastel colors as soon as you step in our lobby. The many artistic ornaments around give a feel of a playful museum-like presence that children instantly recognize and indulge in.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Outside the hotel’s entrance is a tiny terrace that provides a discrete spot for coffee and other short breaks before or after beach journeys. But the real fun is on the rear side of Castellastva’s outside premises. A short walk will lead you through stunning green areas with diverse plants, an outdoor gym area, and an ascension to the on-site playground and an intimate pool terrace for merry pastime. It is this very place that many people consider an alternative to any seaside experience. {% endblocktrans %}
