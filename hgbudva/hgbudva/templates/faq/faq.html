{% trans "Room/apartment reservations:" %}

{% trans "1. How can I book a room/ an apartment through the site?" %}

{% blocktrans %} You can make a reservation easily, in three steps. Click the option: Book now you are accessing the booking process. You need to enter the required information to automatically get an overview of hotel capacity availability. After selecting a hotel and room/apartment, you need to confirm your reservation, enter your personal and card details and book your accommodation.

{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "2. Do I pay a booking fee?" %}

{% blocktrans %} During the reservation process, you need to pay 20 percent of the reservation value via the card. The rest of the value of the arrangement is authorized after the expiration of the free cancellation period, which is in accordance with the cancellation policy (three days until the arrival, out of season, and seven days until arrival in the main season).

{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "3. Is the reservation made through the site guaranteed?" %}

{% blocktrans %}The value of 20 percent of the total price you pay during the booking is a guarantee for the reservation, so you can be sure that you will receive exactly what you booked.

{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "4. What credit cards can I use to pay for the reservation?" %}

{% blocktrans %} You can pay with Visa, Visa electron, Master card and Maestro cards.

{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "5. Can I make a reservation by phone or e-mail?" %}

{% blocktrans %} If you prefer to make a reservation by phone or e-mail, you can do so by sending an inquiry to the email address: {% endblocktrans %} {% trans "reservations@budvanskarivijera.co.me" %} {% blocktrans %} or by calling the phone number: {% endblocktrans %} {% trans " +382 33 402 456." %}

{% trans "6. Is it safe to leave my credit card information on your site?" %}

{% blocktrans %} Your credit card information is sent via a specially designed security server (3D secure). We use the latest and most advanced technology to protect your personal information and guarantee that we will use your information only for the purpose of making a reservation. We have also created a special type of protection for our servers and network against unauthorized access.

{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "7. What are the benefits of booking through the site?" %}

{% blocktrans %} By booking your accommodation through the site, the best price at the time of the search is always guaranteed to you. In addition, you have the opportunity to choose the room/apartment that best suits your affinities and needs. Check our site regularly for information on benefits and discounts when making an online reservation.

{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "8. What if I do not receive an e-mail confirmation after booking?" %}

{% blocktrans %} After booking, you will receive a confirmation email. Please check your spam and junk folders too. If you have not received a confirmation, contact us urgently by e-mail: {% endblocktrans %} {% trans "reservations@budvanskarivijera.co.me" %} {% blocktrans %} or by calling the phone number: {% endblocktrans %} {% trans " +382 33 402 456." %}

{% trans "9. How many rooms/apartments can I book within one reservation?" %}

{% blocktrans %} Within one reservation, you can book several rooms or apartments of a certain type, depending on what you choose.

{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "10. Can I book a room/apartment for the daily stay only? " %}

{% trans "Cancellation:" %}

{% trans "1. How can I cancel a reservation?" %}

{% blocktrans %} You can cancel your reservation free of charge online, three days before arrival in low season, and seven days before arrival at the hotel, in high season. Otherwise, we will apply the penalty payments with the cancellation policy (50 percent or 100 percent of the reservation value is charged, depending on the day of arrival or - no show).

{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "2. How can I change my reservation?" %}

{% blocktrans %} If you want to change the existing reservation, contact us by e-mail: {% endblocktrans %} {% trans "reservations@budvanskarivijera.co.me" %} {% blocktrans %}, or you can correct the date online, by entering your reservation.

{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "3. Do I pay a cancellation fee?" %}

{% blocktrans %}Cancellations are free of charge. However, it is necessary to cancel the reservation in accordance with the policy of the Hotel Group ’’Budvanska Rivijera’’: three days before arrival in low season, and d seven days before arrival in high season (July/August).

{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "4. How do I know that my reservation has been canceled?" %}

{% blocktrans %} After you cancel your reservation, you will receive an e-mail that confirms the cancellation. If you do not receive the e-mail, contact us as soon as possible by e-mail:{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "reservations@budvanskarivijera.co.me" %} {% blocktrans %} or by calling the phone number: {% endblocktrans %} {% trans " +382 33 402 456." %}
