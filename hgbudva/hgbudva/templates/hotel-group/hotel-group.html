{% blocktrans %} Budvanska Rivijera Hotel Group, JSC spread across 5 hotels, is the major tourism company in Montenegro. The long and rich history of providing hospitality services enforces the hotel group’s brand image, for which it is being recognised in the European tourism market. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Breath-taking sites, supreme services, imaginative and delicious cuisine, attentive approach by all hosts, eco and family-friendly business orientation, and the abundance of culture and entertainment events make for an enviable reputation of the Hotel Group. {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% trans "Slovenska Plaza 3* and 4*, 1016 accommodation units" %}
  • {% trans "Aleksandar Hotel 4*, Budva, 187 units" %}

{% blocktrans %} In addition to providing supreme services, all hotels pride themselves with exquisite locations – in the immediate vicinity of sandy, pebble, and concrete beaches – making offers of Budvanska Rivijera even more enticing to travellers who seek both: relaxation and activity. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Leader in the country" %}

{% blocktrans %} Currently, the Hotel Group Budvanska Rivijera enjoys being the largest enterprise of its kind in Montenegro, employing around 200 permanent workers along with seasonal labour expanding the total workforce to more than 600 – all with the aspiration to serve the needs of our guests. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The joint stock company is majority-owned by the state and, as such, makes considerable efforts to ensure a high-quality promotion of the destinations it belongs to.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} By keeping pace with global tourism trends and by listening to demand, all with a view to taking the best possible position in the market and branding its own tourism product, Budvanska Rivijera Hotel Group never stops working on upgrading the quality of its offer through both raising the quality of accommodation facilities and continuous professional training of its human resources.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The greater number of the hotel group’s establishments are seasonal and open from April to November.{% endblocktrans %}
