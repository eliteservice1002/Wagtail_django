{% trans "City Hotel" %}

{% blocktrans %} Hotel Mogren is one of the most authentic buildings in Budva, recently renovated to feature a four-star experience for our guests. Built in 1934, it is an undisputed tourist symbol of the city. It is located opposite of the defensive stone walls of Budva’s Old Town from the Venetian rule and was named after a nearby beach - the most scenic beauty in Budva, Mogren beach. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}The hotel is centrally positioned and close to major attractions in Budva. Within eyesight are: {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% blocktrans %} the main gate to the fortifications of Budva’s Old Town, {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% trans "popular beaches such as " %} Mogren, Mogren II, Slovenska plaza
  • {% blocktrans %} and the city’s marina. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} As soon as you step in, you’ll be able to enjoy a newly decorated reception area with a spacious and opulently furnished lobby. Accessible directly via our lobby is an airy and peaceful atrium that’s centrally positioned and decorated with various plants, a fountain, and bistro tables for the perfect eating or wine tasting experience. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}The terrace functions as a City Café, carrying lyric emotions when looking towards the main square in front of the Old Town, where numerous manifestations, such as the Song of the Mediterranean, the Fish Festival, the Carnival, New Year's Eve Celebration and many other events have been traditionally organized and held.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}From May 20, 2018, the City Café continues to work in new ruins - after the completed adaptation, this cult place of the city of Budva has received a new redesigned appearance while at the same time retaining the traditional Mediterranean feel which had made it distinguishable for years.{% endblocktrans %}
