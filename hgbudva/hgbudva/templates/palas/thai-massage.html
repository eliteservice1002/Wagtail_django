{% trans "Thai massage at Palas Hotel – where East meets the Adriatic Sea" %}

{% blocktrans %}For the first time, Hotel Palas offers a unique and unforgettable experience of the East on the Adriatic Sea. From now on, you can enjoy a Thai massage at the Palas Hotel, which offers a wide range of wellness & spa facilities.

With thousands of years old methods and ancient therapeutic skills, Thai massage will restore energy, get rid of stress, tension and exhaustion. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Traditional Thai massage originates from India, where its use began 25 centuries ago. Buddhist monks in temples kept and perfected it under the influence of other Eastern cultures, especially traditional Chinese and Tibetan medicine. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Thai massage has thus become the most complex healing system, and has been included on the prestigious UNESCO World Heritage List. As a holistic therapy that views man's physical, emotional and spiritual nature as one, it is based on the theory of energy channels that run throughout the body and connect all bodily systems and organs. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The benefit of Thai massage is to bring the body, mind and spirit into a state of balance and harmony that is important for self-healing. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}In the wellness & spa center of the Palas Hotel you can also find other types of massages such as massages for pregnant women, foot massages, back, neck, head and palm massages, volcanic stone massages. Other skin and body treatments are also available to guests.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} In addition to the massage center, the wellness & spa hotel Palas has an indoor pool, two saunas, a jacuzzi, a Turkish bath, adventure showers and a gym. On Mondays and Wednesdays, the youngest guests can attend swimming school. You can get more information about the offer of the wellness & spa center by phone: +382 68 820 773 {% endblocktrans %}
