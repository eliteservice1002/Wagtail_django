{% blocktrans %} Framed in an authentic Mediterranean setting is the Resort Slovenska Plaza, one of the oldest and most sought-after establishments in Budva. It is characterized by romantic aesthetics; low-pitched roofs, symmetrical design, open-air balconies and terraces that leave guests in awe. This family nest incorporates 10 separate villas, all enriched by distinguishable Mediterranean vegetation and named after distinct Montenegrin plant species.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The vastness of the complex creates the impression of walking through a tourist village, set directly on the Adriatic coast of Budva. More so because of the resort being a large sprawling site that pools everything necessary for an all-encompassing vacation: a public car park at the entrance, restaurants, cafes, boutiques, a gallery, tourist agencies, dental and outpatient centres, hairdresser and beauty salons, and much more – literally putting the best of Budva at your fingertips. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Our hotel features two accommodation styles. Rooms & Suites of 3 and 4 stars rating. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Proud winner of ECO Label Certificate" %}

{% blocktrans %} The Resort „Slovenska plaza“ is one of the first winners of the ECO Label Certificate in Montenegro, the European Union certificate for care and preservation of the natural environment. The certificate „ECO Label“ commits the business of the entire complex "Slovenska plaza" (which already stands for Green oasis of Budva) to be based on sustainable development with minimal threat to natural resources. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Sustainability is one of our key corporate values. Therefore, the protection of the environment, waste reduce, and optimization of energy consumption. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Tagged as "A home away from home"' %}

{% blocktrans %} From accommodation and food to recreation and entertainment, Resort Slovenska Plaza combines all requirements to provide one perfect spot for your family summer vacations.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "A place for kids" %}

{% blocktrans %}The youngest guests can enjoy a children’s game room of 150 m² featuring a labyrinth with modern, safe indoor and outdoor play sets. Continuous supervision by friendly and trained staff assures parents of their children’s safety during playtime. Guests also like the fact that entry to this room is free of charge for both kids and their guardians. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} There’s also a special offer for birthday party arrangements where families can rent the whole place and celebrate their special one’s birthday by adding colour and excitement to the occasion.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Entertainment for adult guests" %}

{% blocktrans %}Apart from providing pleasure to children, Resort Slovenska Plaza makes sure not to neglect its adult guests, which is why there are evening theme parties, folklore nights, salsa dance lessons, karaoke singing contests, and more!

Live music programmes are customarily organised at Pjaca restaurant, Rasputin bar, and our resort’s all-inclusive bar. This is where tourists can opt in for all-night fun, away from any disturbance.

For those who prefer physically active holidays and want to explore what’s outside our premises, there’s many picturesque green areas, major attractions in the neighbourhood, beaches, monuments, and museums that can be reached within minutes (hours, at most). Feel free to ask for recommendations at the reception.

NOTE: The resort has a seasonal work schedule; it is open from May to the end of October. {% endblocktrans %}
