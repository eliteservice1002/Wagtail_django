{% trans "Slovenska Plaza - Restaurants" %}

{% blocktrans %} In addition to a main restaurant, the resort Slovenska Plaza offers two more restaurants and three bars. Whether you choose to dine outdoors under palm trees while sitting comfortably on your bistro stool, or eating 5-star dishes inside the chic and classy restaurant adorned with wooden furniture – we got your desires covered. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Main Restaurant" %}

{% blocktrans %}The resort’s Main Restaurant has the capacity of 700 seats, thus bearing semplance to a large food fair with flexibility in food choices due to a catered buffet that allows guests to socialize with people from all over the world, prompt serving size, and tackle their diet goals.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}In addition to its indoor space, the restaurant features a beautiful terrace where you can dine brightened by the natural light of the Sun, or drink your coffee while listening to the gentle sea breeze. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Primorski restoran (The Seaside Restaurant)" %}

{% blocktrans %}Primorski restoran (The Seaside Restaurant) is placed at one of the establishment’s swimming pools and is covered in natural shade, consequently creating a perfect daytime option for rest and indulging in national dishes for true hedonists.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} It has direct access to the pool and is a favourite spot for parents who want their children in their field of vision while at the pool’s playground. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}The restaurant is 80 seats in capacity and is open until 19:00, with a la carte service at fair prices.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Restoran Pjaca (The Square Restaurant)" %}

{% blocktrans %} Restoran Pjaca (The Square Restaurant) is placed in the central square of the development, which it was named after, and offers a high quality a la carte service. Amusement is guaranteed with music programmes each night from 21:00 to 01:00 and traditional folklore programmes several times a week. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The restaurant, the outdoor and indoor part thereof, has 250 seats and works from 09:00 to 01:00. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "BARS" %}

{% trans "Aperitif Bar" %}

{% blocktrans %} Aperitif Bar is an ideal place for refreshment after an intensive day of sports, hiking or sunbathing at the beach. It offers cakes, pastry, and drinks. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}The capacity of this bar is around 40 persons, and it is open from 07:00 to 23:00. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Raspućin (Rasputin)" %}

{% blocktrans %} Raspućin (Rasputin) is one of the most renowned cafés in the entire Budva Riviera. With its 50 seats and an appealing location overlooking the green areas it is considered a favourite meeting place for locals and beyond. Live music, salsa evenings, and fast food at very fair prices are the main trump card of the Rasputin Cafe.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Working hours are from 08:00 to 01:00. {% endblocktrans %}
